Welcome to ShapeShift Gym!

ShapeShift Gym is a home away from home, it was born out of the desire to inspire people to live healthier lives. Our goal is to promote fitness as a way of life, we believe workouts shouldn’t be difficult and boring, and as such our workout routines are infused with fun activities and a great community. We believe the gym should be a happy place where you de-stress after a long day, a place where endorphins are released. We encourage you to take a deep breath when you get in and shut the door to all the noise outside. At ShapeShift Gym you will find rejuvenation, re- awakening and mental stimulation and that is why it is our happy place. We invite you to join us today and share the ShapeShift experience with us. We encourage you to take time of your busy schedules and infuse fitness into your daily schedule. Your body will thank you. Now whether you want to work out alone or as a group, our fitness trainers have got you covered. The good news? Even if you don’t know how to start this fitness journey just walk in to the the gym centre and we will get you started. We pride ourselves in providing excellent service.
